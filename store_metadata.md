## Full Description — English

Master your rhythm with GrooveLab, the ultimate metronome and rhythm training app designed for serious musicians.

PROFESSIONAL METRONOME
- Precise BPM control from 20 to 300
- Multiple time signatures including odd meters
- 6 click sounds: Wood, Digital, Hi-Hat, Clave, Cowbell, Beep
- Customizable accent patterns per beat
- Subdivision support (8ths, 16ths, triplets)
- Swing/shuffle feel (0-100%)
- Human feel mode for natural timing

DRUM PATTERNS
- Built-in drum machine with kick, snare, hi-hat and ride
- 16-step pattern editor
- Per-instrument volume control
- Play along with realistic drum grooves

SETLISTS & LIVE MODE
- Create and organize setlists for rehearsals and gigs
- Save unlimited songs with custom BPM, time signature and settings
- Seamless transitions between songs during live performance
- Auto-advance with countdown

PRACTICE TOOLS
- Speed trainer: gradually increase BPM to build technique
- Practice session timer and history tracking
- Weekly practice goals
- Session statistics and progress charts

RECORDING & TIMING ANALYSIS
- Record your playing and analyze timing precision
- Onset detection shows exactly where your notes land
- Visual heatmap of timing accuracy
- Detailed metrics: accuracy percentage, average deviation

DESIGNED FOR MUSICIANS
- Beautiful dark interface optimized for stage use
- Works offline — no internet required
- All data stored locally on your device
- Available in English, Spanish and Portuguese

Whether you're a drummer, guitarist, bassist, pianist or any musician looking to improve your timing — GrooveLab is your practice partner.
