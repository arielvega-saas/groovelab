GrooveLab - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

Overview

GrooveLab is a metronome and rhythm training app for musicians. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

Data Collection

GrooveLab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All app data — including settings, practice statistics, saved songs, setlists, and recordings — is stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage (SharedPreferences / UserDefaults).

We do not have access to any of your data.

Microphone Access

GrooveLab may request microphone access on native platforms (iOS/Android) to enable the practice recording feature. This allows you to record your playing and analyze your timing precision.

Audio recordings are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any server or third party.

Third-Party Services

GrooveLab does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline (except for font loading on web).

Internet Access

The app may use an internet connection solely for loading display fonts (Google Fonts) on the web version. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Children's Privacy

GrooveLab does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, device encryption). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling GrooveLab from your device. Since no data leaves your device, there is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
